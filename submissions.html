Submissions

We're so excited to hear that you want to enter a submission for us. Our mission relies on the people like you who want to tell your stories. To be clear, here are all of the things that your story will be used for:

  • Your story, written or video, will be posted on the website.
  • You may be used in our live programs.

Note: We will always notify you through the email you send us when your story will be shared.
We will not catch you off-guard.
Notify us if you were not contacted before your media was posted in a format you were not aware of.
